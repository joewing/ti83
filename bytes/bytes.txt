Bytes v2.0 by Joe Wingbermuehle 19990824

>Requirements
Requires a TI-83 or a TI-83 Plus with Ion.

>Installation
For the TI-83: Send bytes.83g to your calculator.
For the TI-83 Plus: Send bytes.8xg to your calculator.

>Object
Get the dots! But don't run into anything including yourself!
Use the arrow keys to move. The menus are there to guide you through
the rest.

>External Levels
The level sets for Bytes are external. The Standard Level set that
comes with Bytes is called ZBYSTD. See the source to ZBYSTD for info
on making Bytes levels.
Thanks to Tim Redmond (Quija13) for making the first level set!
