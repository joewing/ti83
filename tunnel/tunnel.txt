Tunnel v2.0 by Joe Wingbermuehle 19990824

>Requirements
Requires a TI-83 or a TI-83 Plus with Ion.

>Description
This is a simple race-like game.  The object of
this game is simple; stay inside the tunnel.
Use the arrow keys to move, [MODE] to pause, and [DEL] to quit.
